Getting Started with TruLens

TruLens uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models to detect misinformation, sentiment, and key topics in text. Follow these simple steps to verify your content.

1

Copy & Paste

Find a suspicious article, headline, or message WhatsApp forward. Copy the text to your clipboard.

2

Run Analysis

Paste the text into the Dashboard text area and click "Initialize Full Scan".

3

Review Data

Check the Veracity Score (Real vs Fake), Sentiment, and auto-generated summary.

Pro Tip

The extracted keywords can be used to cross-reference other news sources. If a story has "high subjectivity" in the sentiment score, it is more likely to be an opinion piece than a factual report.

Defending Truth in the AI Age

TruLens was born out of a necessity to combat the rising tide of misinformation. With the advent of Generative AI, distinguishing between human-verified facts and machine-hallucinated fiction is harder than ever.

Our mission is to empower users with transparent, explainable AI tools that analyze the linguistic patterns of deception.
